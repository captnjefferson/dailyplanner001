## Summary (from search-surfaced JD; main URL is JS-rendered)
VP of AI Enablement drives AI adoption across the org and enables teams to deliver client
value through intelligent automation. Three core pillars: internal productivity tools,
client-facing innovation, and platform AI optimization.

## Responsibilities (verbatim from surfaced JD)
- Oversee the development and management of an ecosystem of internal applications that
  replace traditional Excel workflows, empowering teams across all crafts — SEO, paid
  social, programmatic display and video, ad ops, strategy and planning, account
  management, and media ops — to work more efficiently and effectively.
- Stay current on AI capabilities within major advertising platforms (Meta, Google, etc.)
  and guide teams on best practices for leveraging platform AI features to maximize
  campaign performance.
- Drive organizational change by building AI literacy, reducing resistance to new tools and
  workflows, and demonstrating the tangible benefits of AI adoption through success stories
  and measurable outcomes.
- Assess AI tools, platforms, and partnership opportunities, making strategic
  recommendations on build vs. buy decisions and technology investments that support the
  AI enablement roadmap.
- Mentor team members and foster a culture of continuous learning, encouraging
  experimentation with AI technologies and developing internal expertise.

## Requirements (verbatim)
- 8+ years of experience in digital marketing, marketing operations, or business transformation
- 3+ years of leadership experience across multiple disciplines
- Excellent knowledge of AI/ML capabilities
