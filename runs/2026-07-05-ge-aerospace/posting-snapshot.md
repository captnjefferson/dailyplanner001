## Role Summary
Leads initiatives enabling the HR function to adopt and apply AI effectively — builds programs, frameworks, and partnerships that translate emerging AI capabilities into practical HR applications across talent acquisition, talent management, learning, workforce planning, and HR operations.

## Essential Responsibilities
- Develop function-wide AI adoption strategy for HR
- Partner with HR leadership to identify high-impact AI opportunities
- Establish evaluation and scaling frameworks for AI solutions
- Prioritize HR AI initiatives aligned with technology roadmaps
- Embed AI into recruiting, talent development, analytics, and operations workflows
- Redesign processes leveraging AI with HR Centers of Excellence
- Support pilots and experimentation with AI tools
- Build AI literacy through training and change enablement
- Coordinate with HR technology, data, and enterprise AI teams
- Establish responsible AI guidelines (fairness, transparency, ethics)
- Define adoption and impact metrics; communicate progress to HR and executive leadership

## Required Qualifications
- Bachelor's (Business, HR, Technology, Data Science, or related)
- Experience leading HR transformation or digital initiatives
- Ability to translate emerging technologies into practical HR applications
- Experience across HR functions; strong stakeholder management
- Understanding of AI technologies and HR applications
- Willingness to travel ≥25%

## Desired Qualifications
- Advanced degree (MBA / Master's HR or Org Psych)
- Experience implementing AI-enabled HR technologies or workforce analytics
- Familiarity with HR tech ecosystems (HRIS, talent platforms, learning systems)
- Knowledge of responsible/ethical AI in workforce decision-making
- Background in regulated industries (aerospace, healthcare, financial services, manufacturing)
