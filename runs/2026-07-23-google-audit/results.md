## Bing/DuckDuckGo hazard (bare name)

Page one includes **"Sheriff: Corsicana Man Fatally Shot Wife, Child, Self"
(CBS Texas)** — a 46-year-old Texas man with the same name, murder-suicide.
Similar age makes it momentarily confusable. Not removable; only crowd-out-able
with fresh indexed content under his name. Also present: Crunchbase (Founder,

## Verdict

Nothing damaging on Google — the problem is **staleness and dilution**. Page one
tells a 2019 story (DC agency web technologist who does photography). The 2026
story — built an AI platform on Claude, 500+ users, runs AI adoption cohorts —
lives only at ranks 6–7. The word "Claude" appears nowhere. The variant searches
are already good; **the bare-name search is the weak surface**, and it's the one
a cold opener runs.
